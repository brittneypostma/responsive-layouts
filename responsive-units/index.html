Viewport Width

I will change based on the width of the viewport.

Viewport Height

I will change based on the height of the viewport.

CH

I will not change in size, I am the size of 2 characters.

EX

I will not change in size, I am the size of 2 of the letter x in the current font.

EM

I will change based on my parent container's font size.

REM

I will always be based off the root font size and will not change unless told to.
