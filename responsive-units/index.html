CH

I am the size of 2 of the "0" character in the current font.

EX

I am the size of 2 of the letter x in the current font.

EM

I will change relative to my parent container's font size.

REM

I will always be based off the root font size and will not change unless told to.

VW

I am 2% of the current viewport's width.

VH

I am 2% of the current viewport's height.

VMIN

I am 2% of the viewport's smaller dimension.

VMAX

I am 2% of the viewport's larger dimension.

Percent

I will change relative to the parent's font size.
